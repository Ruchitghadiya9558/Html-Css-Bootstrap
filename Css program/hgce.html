Hasmukh Goswami College of Engineering

Hasmukh Goswami College of Engineering was established by Shree Monark Education Trust in the year 2007 with an aim to impart quality education in the field of technology and management. The institute aims to provide technical education of the highest standard with the help of state-of-art infrastructure and qualified faculty members. Hasmukh Goswami College of Engineering is imparting value based technical education and developing knowledge, skill, creativity, and personality of budding engineers/professionals, enabling them to meet challenges of the present globalized world and fulfill the demand of the industries.

Welcome to Alpha College

The soul of Khatraj, Alpha College of Engineering & Technology, is established with the principle of nurturing students in the field of technology and innovation in the ambiance of liberty and harmony. Incepted from the roots of Alpha Education Foundation Trust, ACET has been moving at the forefront of advancement for nearly a half a decade, in the area of pivotal technologies and teaching methodologies. Started with 240 seats with the Bachelor Degree in Engineering Programme, the institute now has total intake of 924 in Electronics & Communication Engineering, Computer Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Information Technology and Civil Engineering. The institute also offers a Postgraduate programme in Computer Engineering.
